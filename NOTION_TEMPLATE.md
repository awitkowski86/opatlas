## Pro Tips

### Formatting Best Practices

- **Use H1 for your main headline** - This becomes the page title
- **Use bold for emphasis** - Highlights important points
- **Keep paragraphs short** - 2-3 sentences max for web readability
- **Use emojis sparingly** - Adds personality but don't overdo it
- **Include calls-to-action** - Remind readers what action to take

### Content Tips

1. **Lead with benefits, not features** - "Save 40 hours" vs "Includes auth system"
2. **Be specific with numbers** - "7 days" not "fast", "$29" not "affordable"
3. **Address objections** - FAQ section handles common concerns
4. **Create urgency** - Limited spots, early access pricing, launching soon
5. **One clear CTA** - "Join Waitlist" or "Get Lifetime Access"
